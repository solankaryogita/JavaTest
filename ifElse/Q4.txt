Q4) Write a program to simulate a coin flip and print out "Head" and "Tail" accordingly.
________________________________________________________________________ 

flip=$(($(($RANDOM%10))%2))
if [ $flip -eq 1 ]; then
       echo "heads"
else
      echo "tails"
fi
